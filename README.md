# John Albert Olazo

IT Student | Systems Architecture | Full Stack Development

## Profile

I am a third-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines – Taguig. My approach to software engineering is rooted in a systems-first mindset; I prioritize understanding the underlying architecture and structural integrity of an application over simply achieving functionality.

I treat development as a craft, with a strong focus on:
* **Clean Architecture:** Building modular, maintainable codebases that scale.
* **Security & Reliability:** Implementing robust data flows and secure authentication patterns.
* **Technical Rigor:** Adhering to strict coding standards, type-safety, and performance optimization.

My goal is to bridge the gap between complex infrastructure and intuitive user experiences, evolving into a multi-disciplinary engineer capable of navigating the full lifecycle of a digital product.
